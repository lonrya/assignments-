The Mountains Journal

Here in Utah we have plenty of outdoor activities. Everywhere you go you are surrounded my mountains wiht breathtaking views. Some of the outdoor activities you can do are

  • Hiking
  • Skiing

Hiking

There are thousands of hikes in Utah, some of the best are located within a 30 minute driving distance of Salt Lake City. Some of the better known peaks to hike are

  • Sunset Peak
  • Mount Olympus
  • Mount Superior

A good majority of the hikes in Utah are very difficult. It is recommened to bring plenty of water and make sure you are acclamated to the elevation.

Skiing

Another great outdoor activity in Utah is skiing. There are thousands of mountaons to backcountry ski but if you are looking for resort skiing Utah has plenty of options

  1. Snowbird
  2. Park City

These are two of the more well known ski rsorts in Utah and are excellent choices.
